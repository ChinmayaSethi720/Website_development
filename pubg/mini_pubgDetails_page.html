There are different varities of guns that can be classified into:

  1. Pistols
  2. Assault Rifles
  3. Sniper Rifles





































1. Pistols

  • Pistols are basically hand guns(one handed) that contains 7-10 bullets.
  • Scopes cannot be equipped with these guns.
  • only red dot sight and holographic sight can be equipped to aim with these guns.





















































2. Assault Rifles

  • Automatic weapons that fires 30-47 rounds of bullets are consideres as assault rifle.
  • Sight can be added starting from red dot sight to 6x in these weapons.





















































3. Sniper Rifles

  • These are the only weapons in which 8x(the longest scope) can be attached.
  • These weapons generally have larger damage in comparison to the others.
  • This category also contains the weapon with the greatest damage i.e. AWM that can pierce a level 3 helmet with ease.
